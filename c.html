Herb Information

Scientific Name: Lavandula angustifolia

Uses: Aromatic, culinary (in moderation), essential oils, potpourri, and calming teas.

Ideal Climate: Prefers **full sun** and **well-drained soil**. Thrives in dry, warm climates (Mediterranean-like). Requires **low to moderate watering**.

Key Benefits

  • **Relaxation & Sleep:** Known for its calming fragrance that aids in stress reduction and promoting sleep.
  • **Antiseptic Properties:** Used traditionally to clean minor cuts and scrapes.
  • **Insect Repellent:** Naturally repels moths and other common garden pests.
  • **Aesthetic Value:** Beautiful purple flowers enhance garden appeal.
