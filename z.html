Neem (வேம்பு) Details

Scientific Name: *Azadirachta indica*

Tamil Name: வேம்பு (Vembu)

Parts Used: Leaf, Bark, Root, Flower, Fruit (Panchanga)

Medicinal Properties (Siddha Perspective)

This section would contain detailed medicinal properties as per Siddha texts, for example:

  • **Anti-pyretic:** Helps reduce fevers.
  • **Blood Purifier:** Cleanses the blood, aids in skin disease management.
  • **Anti-septic:** Effective in treating wounds and infections.
  • **Digestive Aid:** Balances Pitta and Kapha doshas.
“As described in Siddha literature **Agathiyar Gunavagadam**”

Cultivation and Care

This section provides practical advice on growing the herb:

  • **Soil:** Prefers well-drained, sandy-loam soil.
  • **Climate:** Thrives in tropical and sub-tropical conditions. Tolerates drought once established.
  • **Watering:** Water regularly when young; mature plants require minimal watering.
  • **Propagation:** Easily propagated through seeds or saplings.
