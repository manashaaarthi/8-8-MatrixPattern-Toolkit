For Cold and Flu Symptoms

Remedy: Thulasi + Thippili + Honey

  • Ingredients: Thulasi (Holy Basil) leaves, Thippili (Long Pepper) powder, Honey.
  • Steps:
    1. Grind a handful of Thulasi leaves and 1/4 tsp of Thippili powder into a paste.
    2. Mix the paste with 1 teaspoon of pure Honey.
    3. Consume twice a day (morning and evening) for 3-5 days.
**Caution Note:** Consult a physician before use if you are pregnant or have chronic health conditions. Do not give Thippili to children under 2 years old.

For Natural Skin Glow

Remedy: Apply Aloe Vera pulp

  • Ingredients: Fresh Aloe Vera leaf pulp (the gel).
  • Steps:
    1. Cut an Aloe Vera leaf and extract the clear, gelatinous pulp.
    2. Gently massage the pulp directly onto the face and neck.
    3. Leave it on for 20-30 minutes, then rinse with cool water.
    4. Apply daily for best results.
**Caution Note:** Perform a patch test on a small area of skin before first use to check for allergic reactions.
